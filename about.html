About EcoCrew

We are a passionate student team working on improving our local park environment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a safer, more engaging, and inclusive playground environment for children and families in our community.

Our Vision

We envision a revitalized local park where children feel excited to play, parents feel confident about safety, and the community feels proud.

Project Goals

  • Improve playground safety standards.
  • Introduce modern and interactive equipment.
  • Encourage physical and cognitive development.
  • Promote teamwork and social skills.
  • Increase community engagement.

Why This Project Matters

Playgrounds are essential spaces for child development. Through this project, we learned how design, safety, and research all work together to improve community environments.
